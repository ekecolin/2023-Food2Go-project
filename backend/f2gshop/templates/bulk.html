Bulking Information

What is bulking?

Bulking is the progressive increase in the number of calories consumed beyond your body’s needs in combination with intense weight training. Bulking is the muscle-gaining phase.

How to bulk?

  • 1. Eat at a caloric surplus.
  • 2. Eat moderate protein and fat, and a substantial amount of carbs.
  • 3. Lift heavy and focus on a moderate rep-range.
  • 4. Eat at a caloric surplus.
  • 5. Avoid “dirty bulking” to avoid excess fat gain.

Some Tips for a successful bulk

  • 1. Prioritize More Carbs to Fuel Hard Training.
  • 2. Tailor your training for bulking.
  • 3. Recover properly.
  • 4. Get Enough Sleep.
  • 5. Eat Enough Protein.
