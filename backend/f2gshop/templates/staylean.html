Lean Information

What is lean?

Being lean means having a low body fat percentage.

How to stay lean?

  • 1. Track Your Macros.
  • 2. Keep Up The Cardio.
  • 3. Know Your TDEE.
  • 4. Eat Properly Around Your Training.
  • 5. Lift Heavy.

Some Tips to stay lean

  • 1. Reduce mindless eating.
  • 2. Be sure to get adequate protein at each meal.
  • 3. Boost your vitamin D intake.
  • 4. Get Enough Sleep.
  • 5. Eat plant-based meals and snacks.
