Weight loss Information

What is weight loss?

It is burning more calories than you take in. The reduction of total body weight.

How to lose weight?

  • 1. Consume fewer calories than your body burns.
  • 2. Eat a healthy diet.
  • 3. Increase physical activity.
  • 4. Stay hydrated.
  • 5. Get enough sleep.

Some tips for weight loss

  • 1. Avoid sugary drinks and processed foods.
  • 2. Include physical activity into your daily routine.
  • 3. Control portion sizes.
  • 4. Avoid skipping meals.
  • 5. Get enough sleep.
